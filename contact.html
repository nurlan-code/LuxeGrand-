Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and policies

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer flexible cancellation policies. For most bookings, you can cancel up to 24 hours before your arrival date for a full refund. Premium packages may have different terms, which will be clearly stated during booking.

Do you offer airport transfer services?

Yes, we provide complimentary airport transfer services for all our guests. Our luxury vehicles and professional chauffeurs ensure a comfortable and stylish arrival to our properties.

Are pets allowed in your hotels?

We welcome well-behaved pets in most of our properties. Please inform us during booking, and we'll ensure your furry companion receives the same luxury treatment as our human guests.

What dining options are available?

Each of our hotels features world-class restaurants with Michelin-starred chefs, offering everything from fine dining to casual cuisine. We also provide 24/7 room service and private dining options.

Do you have meeting and event facilities?

Absolutely! Our hotels feature state-of-the-art meeting rooms, conference facilities, and elegant event spaces perfect for business meetings, weddings, and special celebrations.
